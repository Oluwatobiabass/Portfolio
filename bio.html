Who I Am

Currently enrolled in the ALX Software Engineering Program, I am dedicated to building secure, resilient, and innovative digital solutions. My work is shaped by curiosity, resourcefulness, and a drive to empower organizations with technology that truly makes an impact.

What Makes Me Unique

I approach challenges with a problem-solving mindset, blending cloud technologies, cybersecurity practices, and data insights into solutions that matter. I thrive on adapting quickly, learning continuously, and creating value with every project I take on.

My Passion

Technology is more than a career path for me; it’s my way of solving real-world problems. Cloud engineering allows me to build systems that scale, cybersecurity gives me the tools to safeguard what matters most, and data science fuels my curiosity to find stories and solutions hidden in numbers.

How My Work Enables My Passion

I have deployed and managed Windows instances on AWS EC2, configured and troubleshooted RDP connections, and successfully run Windows-only applications on Linux-hosted cloud environments. I have completed advanced data interpretation projects that provided actionable insights in real-time and gained hands-on experience in secure system administration using PowerShell and DNS configuration. These experiences give me the confidence and technical foundation to pursue larger and more impactful opportunities in technology.

Goals and Aspirations

I aim to gain internships in forward-thinking organizations where I can build secure infrastructures and scalable systems that address Africa’s most pressing digital challenges.

Core Strengths
